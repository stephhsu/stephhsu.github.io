The Gospel

What is the Gospel?

The Gospel is the good news that Jesus Christ, the Son of God, died for our sins, so that we would not have to face the judgement and wrath of God for the wrongs we have committed against Him. Not only that, Christ rose from the dead, triumphant over his enemies, giving those who believe an everlasting joy and hope. However, if we don't believe in this good news, the Bible makes it clear that we will be given the just penalty for our sins, which is eternal suffering in hell where we are eternally seperated from God.

I know that's quite a message to take in! That was but a short summary of the core message of the Bible, which is what we Christians believe to be the truth as God's word. There is much historical evidence pointing to the accuracy of what is written in the Bible.
